About Us

For Vendor App

Mbayat is a local mystery box in Kuwait dedicated to giving exposure to local companies and brands by linking them directly with their targeted audience. Kuwait is full of local brands and companies offering a wide range of products and services across all categories of business.

Mbayat is a new, totally free, marketing channel bridging the gap between those businesses and their targeted audience.

Three simple steps to subscribe to the Mbayat Box

Why would you add your business to Mbayat:
  • Using traditional and conventional advertising methods results in wasted time, effort and resources on people that are outside your targeted audience
  • Nontargeted customers will most likely never turn into potential customers and buyers. With Mbayat you are only linked to your targeted customers.
  • With Mbayat, instead of just hearing about or seeing your product/service, customers will be able to try them firsthand.
  • Promote your products/services using your actual products/services without spending extra on media production or advertising.
  • Reach targeted audience directly without any barriers and without going through any marketing channel.

For User App

Mbayat is a monthly mystery subscription box tailored to your interests. Mbayat is all about helping you explore the market and introducing you to new local brands and business.

By selecting your interests, and subscribing to Mbayat, you will receive a box every month containing items provided by local businesses related to your interests. With three simple steps, subscribe to Mbayat and enjoy the following:

  • Experiencing new products and services related to personal interests at a fraction of the retail price.
  • Staying up to date with the latest trends and business.
  • Enjoying the excitement of receiving a mystery box each month.
  • Sending a Mbayat box as a surprise gift to your friends and loved ones.

Mbayat is a mystery subscription box tailored to costumers’ interest, where you will get the opportunity to try and experience new products and services matching your interest provided by local brands and companies each month.
